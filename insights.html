‚ùå
Wrong Metrics

‚úÖ

üéØ Action Plan

Moving forward, I will:

1

Define Learning Objectives First

For each feature, explicitly state: "This feature exists to test the hypothesis that people want it and need it to be implemented"

2

Use Tiered Development

  • Tier 1 (Validation): Mockups, hardcoded data
  • Tier 2 (Pilot): Functional but non-scalable
  • Tier 3 (Scale): Production-ready
3

Maintain Technical Debt Log

Document shortcuts taken and what would need to change for production, ensuring informed trade-offs

4

Collaborate Closely with Validation Team

Ensure technical decisions are informed by real customer feedback, not assumptions
